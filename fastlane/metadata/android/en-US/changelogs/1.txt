Initial release of PDF Toolkit for F-Droid

Features:
• Complete PDF manipulation suite (merge, split, compress, convert)
• Tesseract OCR for text extraction
• PDF viewer with annotation support
• Image tools (compress, resize, convert, remove metadata)
• Security features (lock/unlock PDFs, watermark)
• Scan to PDF with camera
• 100% offline, privacy-first operation
• No ads, no tracking, no data collection
